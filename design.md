# 🎬 Crunchyroll-Inspired Design System

## Overview

A high-energy, content-forward streaming UI built on a **Cinematic Black canvas** with **Crunchyroll Orange (#ff6400)** as the primary accent. Designed for immersive, low-light viewing with strong visual hierarchy and dense content layouts.

## 🚀 Key Characteristics

* **Cinematic Canvas:** Pure black background for immersive viewing
* **Orange Accents:** Used for CTAs, progress, and highlights
* **Card-Based Layout:** Posters (2:3) & thumbnails (16:9)
* **High Contrast:** Optimized for low-light environments
* **Dense Content:** Designed for binge browsing

---

## 🎯 Design Principles

* Prioritize **content over UI**
* Use **color for hierarchy, not spacing**
* Maintain **sharp edges with minimal rounding**
* Ensure **fast visual scanning**
* Keep **interaction obvious and bold**
